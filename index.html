About

I am a UX Designer and Art Director from Austria living in Berlin. Artworks and illustrations were my gateway to the creative industry which led to the foundation of my own studio and to first steps in the digital world. Art Direction Starting with basic websites several years ago I found myself working as an Art Director for complex projects with a holistic approach soon. Visually appealing designs, subtle details and brand guidelines combined to innovative interfaces across various touch points became my daily companion. Out of this love for aesthetic design my passion for functionality and structure evolved.
